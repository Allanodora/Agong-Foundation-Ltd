About Us

A foundation committed to dignity, education, and community empowerment.

Agong Foundation Ltd is a community-rooted foundation based in Lira, Uganda. The foundation exists to improve the socio-economic situation of girls and women through empowerment and education.

Led by Patron Margaret Odongo, the foundation reflects a deep commitment to service, dignity, opportunity, and long-term community development.

Vision & Mission

Building a stronger future for girls and women.

01

Our Vision

To see girls and women empowered, educated, and able to live with dignity, confidence, and opportunity.

02

Our Mission

To improve the socio-economic situation of girls and women through empowerment and education.

Objectives

Preserving culture, empowering women, and strengthening families.

✓ Promote and preserve cultural heritage through traditional art, festivals, and community participation.
✓ Empower women and girls through education, training, and practical skills development.
✓ Provide counselling and support for social challenges, including domestic violence.
✓ Improve access to health information, including reproductive health and HIV awareness.

Strategic Plan

Three priorities for the next one to three years.

Agong Foundation Ltd will focus its resources on culture, education and skills, and family wellbeing.

Preserve Cultural Heritage

Promote traditional art, cultural festivals, and community knowledge.

Educate and Skill Women and Girls

Expand access to education, workshops, training, and livelihood skills.

Counselling and Social Support

Help women and families address domestic violence and other social challenges.
